Reform Government Vehicles: End Misuse and Ensure Accountability!

The Problem:

Did you know that many government vehicles are misused for personal purposes, wasting taxpayer money? It's time for change!

  • Unnecessary government vehicle movement
  • Personal use of official vehicles for family transportation
  • Exploitation of government cards (MLA/MP cards) to evade tollgate charges
  • Lack of transparency in vehicle identification

The Solution:
- Assign unique, trackable vehicle numbers (e.g., AP 31 1TC 100 for police, AP 31 OT 108 for ambulances) - Implement a centralized database for government vehicles - Ensure accountability for fuel, maintenance, and toll expenses - Prevent sticker tampering and misuse
